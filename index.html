ECHOSCRIBE

Press Ctrl to Record | Release Ctrl to Stop

  1. Make text bold
  2. Copy/Paste
  3. Make size small/normal/large/huge
  4. Make heading 1...6
  5. Align to the center/left/right
  6. Next line/next num lines
  7. Delete text
  8. Previous(last) paragraph/previous(last) num paragraphs
  9. Select previous(last) paragraph/select previous(last) num paragraphs
  10. Next paragraph/next num paragraphs
  11. Select next paragraph/select next num paragraphs
